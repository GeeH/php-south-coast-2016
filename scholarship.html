Ticket Scholarship Programme

We are pleased to announce our Ticket Scholarship Programme for PHP South Coast 2016.

At PHP South Coast, we are committed to including as many people as possible in our conference. This year we are introducing a ticket scholarship scheme to encourage students, under-represented groups and those who can't otherwise afford tickets to experience our events.

I would like to enrol…

To apply for a ticket through the scholarship programme, simply email us on scholarship@phpsouthcoast.co.uk. We will add you to a waiting list, and contact you as soon as we are able to award you with a ticket.

Click here to apply for the Ticket Scholarship Programme

I would like to help…

Simply head to our ticket registration page and donate using the "Ticket Scholarship Donation" ticket type. All ticket donations will be recognised with a thank you during the closing words and on this page, unless you tell us you would like to remain anonymous when donating.

Note: if you wish to donate a single ticket, you must enter £70 when donating. However, any and all donations are gratefully received, regardless of amount.
